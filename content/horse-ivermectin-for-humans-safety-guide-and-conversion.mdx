The question of whether people can take horse ivermectin has become a big topic. Ivermectin is a medicine that fights [parasites](/medical-uses/ivermectin-for-parasites-types-treated-and-effectiveness "Ivermectin for Parasites: Types Treated and Effectiveness"). It has been used for many years to treat [parasites](/medical-uses/ivermectin-for-parasites-types-treated-and-effectiveness) in both animals and people. But it is very important to know the difference between products made for animals, like horse [paste](/dosage-administration/ivermectin-paste-dosage-for-humans-converting-horse-paste-safely), and those made for humans.

Horse ivermectin is not approved for people. The ingredients and doses are made for horses, not for humans. Using horse ivermectin in people can be dangerous.

The main risk is that horse ivermectin has different doses, stronger concentrations, and other ingredients not safe for humans. Taking veterinary ivermectin without a doctor can cause **[overdose](/safety-side-effects/ivermectin-overdose-symptoms-treatment-safe-limits)**, poisonings, and serious health problems. A study by the [World Health Organization (WHO)](https://www.who.int) says ivermectin should only be used in people when a doctor gives it for a real health reason. The World Health Organization warns against using animal drugs without a doctor’s advice because of the risks from wrong doses and impurities that do not meet human safety standards.

In short, ivermectin can be a good medicine for some human diseases. But using horse ivermectin is not safe or right. People should see a healthcare provider and use medications made for humans.

When we compare horse ivermectin and human ivermectin, the active ingredient, ivermectin, is the same. But the purity, doses, and the other ingredients are different, and these differences matter for safety and how well the medicine works.

Human ivermectin is made to be very pure and to have the right amount of the drug for people. Horse paste, on the other hand, is made for big animals like horses, which need higher amounts. The amount of ivermectin in horse paste can be much higher than what is safe for humans. For example, a usual dose of ivermectin in people is about 150–200 micrograms for each kilogram of body weight. Horse doses are often measured in milligrams per kilogram, which is much higher and can cause serious [side effects](/safety-side-effects/ivermectin-side-effects-complete-safety-guide "Ivermectin Side Effects: Complete Safety Guide") if used in people.

Studies show that taking the wrong amount of ivermectin can cause nerve problems. It can make you dizzy, feel sick, or even have seizures or go into a coma. So, understanding how the doses differ is very important to avoid harm.

Many other ingredients in horse ivermectin can also cause problems. Horse paste may contain flavors, like apple or molasses, to make it taste better for horses. These flavors are not tested for safe use in people and could cause allergic reactions or other health issues. There may also be preservatives and stabilizers not found in medicines made for humans. These can cause allergic reactions or irritation in some people.

Understanding how to change the dose of ivermectin from horse product to a human dose is hard and can be risky. Human ivermectin is made to be carefully measured and prescribed by a doctor based on a person’s weight. To convert a horse dose to a human dose, one must do complex math that accounts for the amount of drug in the product, the person’s weight, and the risk of taking too much.

Because of these risks, it is best not to try to dose horse ivermectin yourself. Instead, see a doctor for proper care and use the correct, approved medicine. Human ivermectin products are safe when used as directed.

The [Food and Drug Administration (FDA)](https://www.fda.gov) has clear rules about using animal drugs in people. The U.S. Food and Drug Administration advises strongly against using ivermectin meant for animals in humans. They say animal medicines are not checked for safety or strength in people, and using them can cause serious health issues. The FDA also states that ivermectin can be used in people for certain diseases, but only with products made for humans and with a doctor’s [prescription](/buying-guide/how-to-get-ivermectin-prescription-and-access-guide "How to Get Ivermectin: Prescription and Access Guide").

Many health agencies around the world, including the FDA, say you should talk to a healthcare provider before using any drug off-label. The risks of taking drugs meant for animals are high. Doctors can help make sure the medicine is safe and works well. Human ivermectin products are made to meet safety and health needs for people.

Consider a patient with a parasite infection who is given ivermectin. Using the right medicine made for humans helps the patient get the correct dose. It also lowers the chance of **[side effects](/safety-side-effects/ivermectin-side-effects-complete-safety-guide)** and helps the treatment work well.

In summary, while ivermectin is important for treating some health issues, it is very important to use the forms made for humans. Only use it if a doctor prescribes it. This keeps the patient safe and helps the medicine work right.
